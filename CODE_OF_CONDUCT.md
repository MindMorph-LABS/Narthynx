# Code of Conduct

Narthynx contributors are expected to keep the project respectful, practical, and focused on building reliable open-source infrastructure.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical risk.
- Keep discussions grounded in code, tests, and project goals.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors who violate these expectations.
