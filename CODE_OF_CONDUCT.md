# Code of Conduct

Narthynx contributors are expected to keep the project respectful, practical, and focused on reliable open-source infrastructure.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical risk.
- Keep discussions grounded in code, tests, safety behavior, and project goals.
- Give actionable feedback and explain product-identity concerns clearly.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or dismissive behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, private keys, or sensitive production data.
- Encouraging unsafe automation or bypassing Narthynx policy and approval gates.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions away from the project scope.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues or pull requests, restrict participation, or ban contributors who violate these expectations.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact the maintainers privately and include only the information needed to understand the situation.
